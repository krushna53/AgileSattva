Make Collaboration Easy Again

Even the most well-intentioned people struggle to work with each other in a team. Just like musicians need to play in harmony to create beautiful music, team members need to overcome their hidden personal and group barriers to work well together. When they don't, the result can be as jarring as an orchestra playing out of tune.

Our Collaborative Excellence program addresses these unconscious dynamics at three levels: individual mindsets, interpersonal interactions, and dynamics of a group-as-a-whole. This comprehensive approach creates the foundation for true team success.

Common Questions

Unlike traditional team building, we focus on understanding and addressing the underlying patterns that affect team performance towards common goals. Our approach creates sustainable behavioral change rather than temporary enthusiasm.

The program typically involves 2-3 days of workshops followed by implementation support. We can customize the schedule based on your team's needs and availability.

An indicator of success is that teams develop an awareness of their underlying patterns and come together to develop a strategy to work together - enhancing collaboration and self organization. Additionally, we use a combination of quantitative metrics (team velocity, collaboration scores) and qualitative feedback to track improvements in team dynamics and performance.
